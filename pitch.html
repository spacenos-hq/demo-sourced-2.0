03 · The Resolution

Post once. AI closes the deal.

Sourced replaces phone-tag procurement with an autonomous agent that sources, calls, compares, negotiates, and closes.

Slide 10 · The Solution
Autonomous procurement in under 24 hours.
01 · Post a Requirement: speak or type in 60 seconds; AI structures the RFQ.
02 · AI Finds Suppliers: checks platform then scrapes Google Maps for every relevant supplier.
03 · Automated Outreach: email + AI phone call; “press 1 to participate” (TCPA compliant).
04 · All Bids in One Place: platform, email, phone responses compared side-by-side.
05 · AI Negotiates: optimal split-bid + re-engagement calls to sharpen pricing.
06 · Close: contractor taps Confirm; 12–22% average savings.
