04 · Roadmap

Three phases. 18 months.
Each phase ships value.

Phase-gated execution. Revenue at every gate. No big-bang launches.

Phase 01 Foundation (Months 1–7) — Rhode Island Pilot
▾

Contractor app + supplier portal. Prove 3+ bids per RFQ. Off-platform supplier discovery and AI calling are in the foundation, not “later.” Target: $22K MRR.

Phase 02 Growth Engine (Months 7–12) — SEO Flywheel
▾

Auto-generated SEO supplier pages + autoblog. Organic contractor inbound at near-zero CAC. Supplier claim flow converts on proven demand. Target: $116K+ ARR run rate.

Phase 03 Full Platform (Months 12–18) — Payments + Data
▾

Escrow payments + transaction fees. Pricing data API. Enterprise accounts. Target: $500K+ ARR and Series A readiness at Month 18.
